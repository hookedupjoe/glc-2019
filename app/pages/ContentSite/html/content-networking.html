2019 Networking Activities

Networking Activities Available Through The Greenbrier

In addition to the conference program, attendees may take advantage of the many networking opportunities available at The Greenbrier, such as bowling, bunker tour, falconry, fishing, golf, horseback riding, mountain biking, spa, tennis, and trapshooting. Be sure to mention that you are part of the GLC block, as time slots have been reserved for our group until September 1.


How to Register

Reservations for these activities may be made by calling The Greenbrier Reservation Center at (855) 346-7156 ext. 1. Additional fees apply and will be billed to your hotel room. If cancelling within 24 hours, you will be billed for the full cost of the activity.
